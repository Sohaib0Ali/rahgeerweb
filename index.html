Welcome to Rahgeer-e-Harmain

Rahgeer-e-Harmain is your trusted companion on the sacred journey of Umrah and Hajj. We are dedicated to providing Pakistani pilgrims with a comprehensive, real-time guidance and support system throughout their pilgrimage. Our mobile application is designed to ensure that every aspect of your journey, from preparation to completion, is smooth, organized, and spiritually fulfilling.

Benefits of Rahgeer-e-Harmain

Real Time Guidance

Get step-by-step instructions and guidance throughout your Umrah or Hajj journey, ensuring you perform every ritual correctly and with ease.

Weather and Crowd Alerts

Stay informed with real-time updates on weather conditions and crowd density, helping you navigate your pilgrimage safely.

Comprehensive Support

Access essential tools and checklists that help you prepare, organize, and manage your entire journey from start to finish.

Safety and Security

Share your location with loved ones for added peace of mind, knowing they can track your journey in real-time.

Spiritual Enrichment

Deepen your spiritual experience with educational content that enhances your understanding of each ritual.

Convenient Services

Easily connect with local service providers for accommodations, transportation, and shopping needs, making your trip smoother.

Language and Currency Support

Use in-app language translation and currency conversion tools to overcome language barriers and manage your finances with ease.

Community Connection

Engage with a community of fellow pilgrims, sharing experiences, tips, and support throughout your journey.

Who Are We?

Who Are We

Rahgeer-e-Harmain is a comprehensive mobile application designed to assist pilgrims on their spiritual journey to Makkah and Madinah. Our mission is to provide real-time guidance, alerts, and support throughout the entire pilgrimage, ensuring a safe and fulfilling experience.

Our Story

Born from the passion to ease the pilgrimage experience, Rahgeer-e-Harmain is the result of dedicated efforts to integrate technology with the spiritual journey. Our application is designed to be your trusted companion, offering everything from route guidance to weather alerts.
