Welcome to Rahgeer-e-Harmain

Rahgeer-e-Harmain is your trusted companion on the sacred journey of Umrah and Hajj. We are dedicated to providing Pakistani pilgrims with a comprehensive, real-time guidance and support system throughout their pilgrimage. Our mobile application is designed to ensure that every aspect of your journey, from preparation to completion, is smooth, organized, and spiritually fulfilling.

Major Features

Guided Ritual Assistance

Detailed instructions and videos for each ritual, ensuring you perform them correctly and confidently.

Real Time Location Sharing

Share your location with your group instantly to enhance safety and coordination during your journey.

Live Updates

Get real-time information on weather and crowd conditions to plan your activities and avoid delays.

Language Translation

Communicate effortlessly with fellow pilgrims from around the world using our integrated language translator.

Mentorship Lectures

Access recorded lectures for in-depth guidance, mentorship, and application usage tutorials at your convenience.

Customizable Packing Tool

Create and manage your packing list with our tool to ensure you have all the essentials for your trip.

Currency Converter

Easily convert currencies to better manage your budget and plan your journey according to your financial resources.

Who Are We ?

Who Are We

Rahgeer-e-Harmain is a comprehensive mobile application designed to assist pilgrims on their spiritual journey to Makkah and Madinah. Our mission is to provide real-time guidance, alerts, and support throughout the entire pilgrimage, ensuring a safe and fulfilling experience.

Our Story

Born from the passion to ease the pilgrimage experience, Rahgeer-e-Harmain is the result of dedicated efforts to integrate technology with the spiritual journey. Our application is designed to be your trusted companion, offering everything from route guidance to weather alerts.
